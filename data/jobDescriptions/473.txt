PRIMARY PURPOSE OF POSITION

Protection of the Company's assets to include our self-insured retention of
$3 Million through the execution and management of field investigations of bodily injury, property damage and company damage incidents to include high profile cases with significant monetary exposure.  Establish appropriate reserves on cases in accordance with established accounting principles. Analyze liability and evaluate financial risk to the company in high complexity injury and large property damage losses. Make presentations to Senior Management regarding investigations with high impact potential.  BGE Claims does not have a Manager Claims Case Management position. Prepare written reports and case analysis as required. Support the loss prevention process with analytical data.

Note: Position may be required to BGE work extended hours, including 24x7 coverage for storms or other energy delivery emergencies.


PRIMARY DUTIES AND ACCOUNTABILITIES

*Accountability

- Perform and coordinate field investigation of property damage, bodily injury, automobile accidents, environmental and company damage incidents that involve liability issues for the company. Respond to and coordinate the investigation of major incidents (gas explosions, fires, electrocutions) including significant company damage events on a 24-hour basis. Meet with customers/claimants, witnesses, etc. to obtain facts of incidents. Evaluate damages, make liability determinations, analyze losses and where warranted develop a settlement strategy and negotiate a cost effective settlement. Retain experts, lead and conduct site inspections with experts and company counsel. Coordinate and manage litigation brought against the company.

- Prepare written investigation reports, letters and other documents including discovery responses.

- Provide 24/7 on call emergency response

- Provide educational services to internal and external constituencies.

- Assist in the training of new E02 Claims Case Managers.

- Serve on root cause investigation teams to provide the Claims viewpoint in these activities.

POSITION SPECIFICATIONS

Minimum:

- Bachelors degree and minimum 5-8 years of relevant investigative, claims or insurance experience, OR in lieu of a Bachelor's degree, minimum 9-12 years of business experience, at least 8 of which must be relevant investigative, claims or insurance experience.
Preferred:

-10 years of relevant investigative, claims or insurance experience.
Qualifications

- Independent settlement authority of $25,000.

- Protection of company assets (through proper investigations)

- Minimize Company Liability exposures (effective settlements and claim denials)

- Customer/client satisfaction while balancing effective claim performance

- Contribute to company performance through amounts collected (Financial Recovery field investigations).

- Effective financial management (adequate reserving and payments)

- Self motivated requiring minimal supervision and an ability to handle a heavy case load.
