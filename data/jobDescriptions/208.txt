Position Purpose:

Provide Legal advice and services including advising management concerning State and Federal legislation, regulations and other requirements applicable to Centene business activities and initiatives
Serve as a lead resource to Centene departments and entities for research and interpretation of specific healthcare policies and regulations
Provide legal advice to Centene entities to define and develop solutions to regulatory requirements that impact various product lines and programs
Review regulations, RFPs and other documents affecting such activities, and identifying and appropriately responding to regulatory risks or opportunities.
Support Government Relations and Compliance departments by assessing and translating complex legislation, rules and regulations into understandable information and facilitating analysis of compliance and implementation implications
Review, draft and negotiate contracts, policies and procedures and related documents
Manage outside counsel and direct, monitor and develop legal strategies in connection with litigation and arbitration disputes
Direct and conduct training and preventative law activities
Act as a lead Corporate Counsel on projects or certain matters

Education/Experience: Graduate of an accredited law school and admitted into a state bar. 7+ years of experience in the areas of corporate or health law. Experience with government contract law, health law and managed care preferred. Knowledge of Medicare and/or Medicaid insurance, state insurance and HMO regulations preferred. For roles involving Litigation only, Litigation experience is required.



License/Certification: State Bar and currently in good standing.
