JOB DESCRIPTION
Description

As a Senior Accounting Manager at Caterpillar, you will the opportunity to play a critical role in the development and implementation of unit financial and product costing strategies.


Job Duties/Responsibilities may include, but are not limited to:

* Managing a team of management staff
* Providing a strategic focus and leading initiatives on complex financial, product and/or pricing issues
* Participating in the development and support of business strategies
* Communicating effectively within the team and with internal customers and data providers, including presentations to all levels of management
* Proactively interacting with customers to develop, understand and manage customer requirements and expectations
* Providing technical guidance and expertise on business issues of global impact and/or high complexity such as financial reporting requirements
* Leading initiatives and making decisions outside the team to represent the group
* Collaborating with internal and external parties as appropriate
* Providing creative solutions that challenge traditional methods and processes

* Evaluating and reporting on staff performance, participating in employee selection, providing career guidance and training direction, complying with personnel practices, and promoting team awards and successes


Qualifications

Basic Qualifications:

* Bachelor's level degree, preferably in accounting
* 7 to 10 years of progressively responsible job-related experience

Top Candidates Will Also Have:

* Prior supervisory experience
* Must demonstrate high levels of initiative, leadership, good judgment and superior communication skills
* Strong analytical, process improvement, problem solving and human relations skills

* Prior Caterpillar experience desirable, especially PSLD and/or component finance experience
