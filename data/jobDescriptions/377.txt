Finance



The Finance department, which manages the company’s budgets, forecasts and investments, and provides analysis of our financial performance and strategies.



Job overview and responsibilities



This position supervises the monthly close process and preparation of consolidated financial statements and internal reporting packages, among other responsibilities as outlined below.

Oversee month end close activities, including:
   – journal entry preparation and review
   – preparation of Income Statement/Balance Sheet/Cash Flow Statement
Ensure financial statements are accurate and complete
Support frequent flyer accounting and loyalty program legal entity accounting
Review of monthly balance sheet account reconciliations
Record and perform analysis over health and welfare plan monthly accounting
Plan and coordinate employee benefit plan audits and preparation of associated financial statements
Plan and coordinate annual subsidiary audits and financial statement preparation
Coordinate annual CRAF Rate setting process
Plan and coordinate annual Intangible asset impairment testing
Prepare quarterly Department of Transportation Form 41 Reporting
Oversee other miscellaneous government reporting, primarily Census Bureau requests
Manage financial reporting staff and provide development opportunities
Respond to internal department requests
