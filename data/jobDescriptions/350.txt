Description

Underground storage tank current regulations;
Surface Water Pollution Prevention Plan (SWPPP);
Regulated waste management/disposal;
Pollution prevention best management practices;
Understand and oversee program permit renewals.
Strong analytical, time management, organizational and computer skills (Word, PowerPoint, Excel, Acrobat);
