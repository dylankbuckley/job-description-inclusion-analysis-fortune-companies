Microsoft Tax & Trade Department is responsible for Worldwide Tax & Trade Reporting, Compliance, and Planning, including the technology and process functions to support the coordination of the worldwide tax and trade functions.  There are seven major functions within the Tax Department.  1) Tax Affairs; 2) International Tax and Transfer Pricing; 3) Tax Reporting 4) Tax Planning and Tax Controversies; 5) Indirect Tax; 6) Tax Litigation and 7) Tax Process, Technology & Trade.

Responsibilities
Responsibilities:

Assist with preparation of Microsoft’s US GAAP income tax accounting models including deferred accounting, payable accounting, tax accounts roll-forward, FIN48, return-to-provision true-up, SBC and ETR
Provide support with building the processes and calendar to ensure a smooth connection among accounting, tax compliance and tax accounting functions
Build excellent audit files to support financial statement audits
Provide audit support to the Tax Controversies group for issues related to US income tax positions and filings
Assistance with maintenance and compliance of key SOX controls related to US tax accounting
Scrutinize, challenge, and propose changes to existing processes and procedures
Keep up to date with tax legislation, SEC Regulations, and US GAAP changes, practice and global developments

Qualifications
Minimum Qualifications:



BS in Accounting or MSA, MST, or MAC
5+ years of experience of applicable experience
Deep hands-on experience with ASC740 modeling and tax accounting software
Hands-on experience with IRS US and US international corporate income tax
Strong database and desktop application ability (specifically SAP and MS Office)
Strong project management skills
Excellent communication and interpersonal skills, ability to collaborate effectively with team members and business stakeholders
Exhibit analytical thinking, ability to work in a fast-paced environment with excellent prioritization and organizational skills
Preferred Qualifications:

CPA certification is desired
Previous experience in corporate tax department;
Previous experience in Big 4 corporate tax practices
