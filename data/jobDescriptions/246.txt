DESCRIPTION
Are you a software engineer with strong Python or Scala skills that has strong interest in Machine Learning and Data Science? Procter & Gamble is looking for a software engineer with strong interests in these areas to work closely with our Data Scientists on exciting business use cases primarily in the retail and marketing area. In this role you would be working closely with data scientists to help them scale their algorithms.
KEY RESPONSIBILITIES & DUTIES:
As a Software Engineer working with Data Science at P&G, your key responsibilities will include:
Code refactoring with Data Science and Data Engineering to get machine learning models from proof of concept to production
Infrastructure engineering, security engineering, and architecture planning for new systems
Develop internal tooling and libraries needed to support machine learning efforts
Regular innovation/proof of concept work to deliver breakthroughs vs current techniques
