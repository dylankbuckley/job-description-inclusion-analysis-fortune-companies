Summary:

This position will work in the Contract Administration Group reporting to the Contract Administration Manager. The administrator is the initial point of contact within the contracts group ensuring that deals are appropriately papered, contract provisions are adhered to, and business records are maintained. The administrator must communicate effectively in a cross-functional environment on matters relating to systems and contracts.


Essential Duties and Responsibilities:



Support ET Entities Commercial by providing value added services such as:

Maintenance of a database with timely contract information
Interface with Legal and coordinate all contract/amendment and
termination requests
Manage contract inventory
Advise dealmakers of notice provision and flow information relative to
the contract inventory
Facilitate contract execution
Facilitate contract assignments
Draft all contract notifications, including name changes/outages/etc.
Maintain master data in various systems as required
Handle Regulatory requirements associated with these entities as required
Process documentation required for daily/monthly trading activity
Present information effectively to various groups or management when required
Assist lower level team members with inquiries and work processes
Analysis of data as required
Negotiate non critical items
Special projects as required, including cross training with other team members
And any additional duties as assigned from time to time
