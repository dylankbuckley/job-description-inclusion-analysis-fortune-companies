The Associate Finance Director is responsible for various financial functions including business analytics, metrics reporting and ad-hoc analysis. This person will also create in-depth financial models and analysis of company financial and operational metrics to evaluate and influence numerous aspects of the company's business performance.

Primary Responsibilities:

Lead the financial management of ORx IT SG&A expense and develop metrics, benchmarks and analytics to guide the appropriate investments in alignment with Enterprise product strategy
Lead and mentor team of Analysts
Lead and Manage quarterly IT forecast process and work cross functionally to ensure alignment with Optum Tech
Partner with business, IT, and finance leaders to understand and control SG&A expenses
Review Capital investments and track benefits
Partner with accounting on month end close
Partner with BU & segment finance to ensure cost management and investments are reasonable and aligned with defined performance targets and financial thresholds
Analyze and synthesize IT operating expense trends to identify savings opportunities and collaborate with cross functional teams to drive progress against these opportunities
Proactively seek opportunities to improve processes and identify areas of improvement
Work closely with the business to develop, recommend and establish strategies, plans, and processes to improve profitability and cost efficiencies
Lead development of business / financial case for specific initiatives, capital investments and new business opportunities
Assist in prioritization of investment for the business, providing recommendations based on financial and non-financial targets
Act as subject matter expert on the IT supported segment & apply in-depth knowledge of the business for recommendations to management
Perform Enterprise Cost Management reports for month-end close and quarterly forecasting on behalf of the supported segment
Complete standard variance analysis and research variance drivers and explain cause-effect relationships
Analyze Workforce / FTE trends and enhance FTE reporting and insights through a closer partnership with business units during forecast cycles
