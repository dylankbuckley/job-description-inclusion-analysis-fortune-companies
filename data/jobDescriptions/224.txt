Performing repairs and preventative maintenance on a wide variety of building and facility equipment, fixtures and systems.
Installing, troubleshooting, repairing and maintaining a wide variety of facility plumbing, mechanical, electrical, heating and cooling systems.
Conducting preventative maintenance inspections and performs required preventative maintenance duties.

Qualifications


 This is an entry level position and will be compensated accordingly. You must possess the below minimum qualifications to be initially considered for this position. Preferred qualifications are in addition to the minimum requirements and are considered a plus factor in identifying top candidates. Experience would be obtained through a combination of prior education level classes, and current level school classes, projects, research and relevant previous job and/or internship experience.

Minimum Qualifications:

This U.S. position is open to U.S. Workers Only. A U.S. Worker is someone who is either a U.S. Citizen, U.S. National, U.S. Lawful Permanent Resident, or a person granted Refugee or Asylum status by the U.S. Government. Intel will not sponsor a foreign national for this position.
Must be willing to work in a clean room environment, wearing coveralls, hoods, booties, safety glasses and gloves.
Must be willing to lift up to 30 lbs, pull 250lbs at least once a day and spend the majority of time (greater than 90% less break time) on the floor working on tool sets, walking 3-5 miles a day, and/or standing/sitting for 9 hours.
Candidate must be willing to work a compressed work week schedule ' Shift is twelve hours long and rotates from three to four days a week.
The candidate must possess at least one of the following educational or experience requirements:
Associate of Arts or Associate of Science degree in Mechanical Technology or related field with 3+ years of experience
Certification in any STEM or any other technical field of study.
Military Experience considered in lieu of degree.
Preferred Qualifications:

Experience with industrial mechanical systems operations and/or vibration analysis a plus.
Role modeling safety behaviors and influencing team members.
Proficiency in Maximo and CDOCS, ILM a plus.
