The Finance Manager will work alongside the business partners in VMware’s Products Division. The Finance Manager will provide financial advice, planning, and analysis to the business partner and his/her staff that helps the business meet or exceed goals. Among the areas of most active engagement,

• BU Finance lead for FP&A integration activities
• Bookings/Revenue actuals & plans models
• R&D investment decisions
• Inbound licensing/COGS FP&A
• Marketing programs, business trends and pricing analysis
• Spend & HC analysis and review
• Location strategy
• Acquisitions: financial due diligence and integration
• Business Unit pro forma statements
• Short & long-term planning processes
• Capex, Deferred revenue and other analysis
• Lead one or more of the critical financial planning & analysis processes for the Products Division. Examples include the inbound licensing framework and the investment/portfolio analysis process. Create, improve, or streamline the process as needed. Champion the framework and drive adoption within FP&A and the Products Division.
• Collaborate with the Products FP&A team and other parts of VMware Finance to enhance the function’s contribution to the company. Share best practices, identify gaps, drive change and help determine priorities.

Requirements:

• BA or BS in Business, Finance or Accounting; MBA preferred
• 5 - 8+ years of experience in finance/accounting with a strong record of career progression
• Experience working in an enterprise software, SaaS, or other hi-tech company
• Demonstrated results providing finance business partnering to R&D/Product Management executives
• Strong understanding of accounting and compliance requirements
• Ability to build analytical models that can be driven into standard planning tools
• Experience with Microsoft Excel, and Oracle Financials reports

Desired Skills:

• CPA or equivalent experience
• Strong and independent thinker and influencer
• Track record of showing finance leadership in a fast-paced, high-growth environment
• Strong organizational, problem solving and analytical skills
• Strong initiative and collaborative work-style
• Strength in succinctly explaining complex transactions and business impact
• Ability to drive process-based improvement across functions and within Finance
• Integrity
