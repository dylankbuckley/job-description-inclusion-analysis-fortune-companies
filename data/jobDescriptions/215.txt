The Corporate Finance & Accounting (F&A) Department is seeking qualified undergraduate and graduate students for an enterprise transformation project. This internship assignment is designed to immerse interns in the corporate culture and expose them to interactions with management. Interns participate in business meetings and deliver presentations to senior management. If you're looking for an exciting internship with competitive pay, valuable networking opportunities in our corporate office, and meaningful evaluations and feedback, we want to hear from you!

Responsibilities and Duties

Internship opportunities are available in the following F&A departments:

Global Accounting Center (GAC)

Performs monthly reviews of balance sheet accounts and existing accounting procedures for completeness, as well as US GAAP and local GAAP compliance
Participates in multiple accounting projects
Creates monthly or ad hoc financial analysis and accounting reports
Corporate Financial Reports and Plans (FR&P)

Manages components from Corporate Accounting, External Reporting, Reporting & Consolidations, Corporate Planning, and Financial Information Systems (FIS)
Assists with a variety of challenges in different F&A areas, including accounting, financial analysis, reporting and planning
Knowledge and Skills

Rising college junior or senior, or recent graduate within the last 24 months
Available to work 30-40 hours per week
Experience with Microsoft Office products (Excel, Word, PowerPoint, Access) or equivalent software
Detail-oriented with effective time management skills
Strong communication skills, both written and verbal
