Job Description & Qualifications

Our Chief Privacy Officer (CPO) will lead and accelerate Ford’s drive to use data effectively and responsibly as the world’s most trusted company. The role requires expertise in privacy compliance, project management, and digital technology. It also requires strong, collaborative relationships with other partners in a large and sophisticated organization.

Responsibilities
The CPO will lead Ford’s global corporate privacy program across all business units. Responsibilities will include:

Chair an executive board that sets policies for responsible use of data and oversees an effective data governance system
Lead a system for embedding privacy by design in Ford’s culture and ensuring adherence to policies for responsible use of data
Coordinate with senior management, the Compliance Office, regional data protection officers, the chief information security officer, and the General Auditor’s Office to maintain and improve controls, use audits to drive improvements, conduct training, and lead vendors with respect to data protection and privacy
Work with the Office of the General Counsel to stay current in legal requirements, manage risk, and implement changes to align with evolving requirements in all markets
Actively engage in external privacy forums to understand trends, learn best practices, and influence public policy
Lead a cross-functional incident response team and data breach preparedness
Validate, monitor and continuously improve controls to protect data from unauthorized access and use while ensuring efficient access for proper uses
Work with our Communications team to articulate Ford’s privacy principles both internally and externally in a consumer-centric way, including with regulators and NGOs
Serve as the voice of Ford’s customers, dealers and suppliers with respect to effective and responsible use and sharing of data
Passionately and proactively find ways to improve the customer experience and build trust in Ford
Work with the Office of the General Counsel to respond to inquiries from regulators, enforcement agencies, and data protection authorities
Conduct privacy impact assessments to analyze risks in new or changed business activities
Establish and lead measures for mitigating risks related to collection and use of data while improving the company’s ability to innovate and serve its customers
Monitor the effectiveness of privacy-related risk mitigation and compliance measures
Provide strategic guidance to corporate officers regarding resources and technology to support privacy
Develop and implement training initiatives to improve the knowledge and understanding of data privacy by Ford personnel
Engage with Ford’s Government Affairs team to help shape future data privacy laws
Basic Qualifications

Bachelor’s Degree
10+ years of experience in information technology, data analytics, compliance, program management and/or privacy in positions of increasing responsibility
5+ years of experience in a supervisory role
Preferred Qualifications

IAPP certification or deep knowledge of global privacy practices, including industry-specific regulations and general technology solutions, security standards and frameworks
Demonstrated ability to manage effectively complex and significant privacy and information security incidents and matters
Proven experience articulating privacy across a multi-national organization, creating awareness, and driving adoption across diverse business units
Demonstrated organization, facilitation, communication, and presentation skills
Ability to lead a team in a fast-paced environment
Highly collaborative individual with ability to influence others and build strong professional relationships, including with technology, business, and legal partners
Strong ability to navigate through barriers to drive successful initiatives throughout the business
Deep experience managing high-stakes issues in a professional, assertive, and proactive manner
Strong organizational skills and ability to handle multiple, cross-functional projects; able to work independently and as part of a team in a fast-paced environment
