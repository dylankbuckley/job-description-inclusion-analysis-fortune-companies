Background on the Position
The Wealth Management Tax and Client Reporting Operations (TCRO) team is seeking an Operations Associate to provide support and production of monthly and quarterly performance reports for Financial Advisors and their Institutional and ultra-high net worth clients. He/she will work closely with Financial Advisors (FAs) and other Client Reporting Operations associates on solving performance measurement discrepancies, maintaining ongoing quality reviews for all performance reporting, and effectively managing expectations. The candidate will report to the Client Reporting Team Lead.

Primary Responsibilities
-Research, reconcile and update monthly activity by using monthly custodian statements as well as in-house custody data
-Set up and maintain appropriate comparisons with market indexes and customized benchmarks
-Respond to technical questions concerning client accounts and manager performance statistics
-Manage the reporting expectations of high profile Financial Analysts for custom reporting
-Prepare monthly, quarterly, and ad-hoc performance reports
-Ensure index, product attributes, and performance numbers are valid
-Participate in team projects and initiatives to maximize use of strategic reporting solutions in performance operations
