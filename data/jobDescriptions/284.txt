Description:This position supports the Vice President, F-35 GM and Vice President, F-35 Deputy GM. In this capacity, employee performs a variety of administrative duties in support of the F-35 LOB executive team. This position will require performance of the following tasks:

• Manages various office functions
• Interviews callers making proper referrals
• Manages flow and filing of sensitive and proprietary information
• Arranges meetings and conferences
• Acts as the F-35 complex conference room coordinator/scheduler and ensures all the computer equipment is up and running; handles issue resolution
• Receives, refers, or answers mail, to include review and properly re-directing e-mail with appropriate follow-up
• Telephone coverage is critical in this function and requires constant monitoring and accessibility for phone coverage, including weekends
• Uses personal computers and standard desktop software to compose reports, correspondence, and memorandums
• Reviews drafts and finished documents for appropriate grammatical usage
• May provide guidance and direction, as needed, to lower-level administrative professionals and other clerical employees
• Professionalism and highly effective interpersonal skills are a requirement in this position due to the level of Corporate and company leadership interface with both offices, in addition to external dignitaries and civic leaders
• Event support and coordination is required to support offices with company tours and special events
• Coordination is critical in this function to ensure organizations are kept informed of emerging issues and information
• Able to maintain confidential information pertaining to F-35 operations, proprietary, and personnel matters.
• Manage very complex and intense calendar of events/schedule.
• Able to manage Offsite/Special Event planning
• Capable of delivering basic Audio/Visual Support for Meetings
• Ability to handle basic budget management
• Must be able to appropriately order office supplies
• Must be able to work flexible hours and overtime including evenings and weekends as needed
Basic Qualifications:
• Experience as an Administrative Assistance with 5+ years experience.
• Proficient with Microsoft Office - Microsoft Word, Power Point, Outlook, Excel. (Must be able to create, edit, and manipulate all major applications. Must be able to do pivot tables in Excel.)
• Experience arranging international/domestic travel and expense reporting.
• Experience managing outlook calendar and email for leaders.
Desired Skills:
• Must possess excellent interpersonal skills, and the ability to work well with all levels of staff, as well as external contacts including high levels of Company/Government personnel
• Proficient with Travel/Expense reporting tools and reconciling expense reports; familiarization with Concur Travel/Expense Reporting system
• Experience arranging international/domestic travel
• Experience working with sensitive information, a high level of confidentiality is required
• Detail oriented with ability to multi-task in a fast-paced, changing environment
• Advanced experience in Microsoft Office applications such as PowerPoint, Word, Excel, and Access. Able to do pivot tables in Excel
• High level of organizational and prioritization skills
