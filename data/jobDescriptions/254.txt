Job Description
ACCOUNTABILITY:
The centralized Accounts Receivable Admin will focus on Market Unit loss lines to ensure accuracy and completeness of financial administration key controls, identify/correct exceptions and non-compliance, as well as highlight opportunities for improvement that will help drive a sustained and well controlled environment. This position will oversee short pay details, manage location disputes, unauthorized charges, customer refunds, and customer payments.

KEY ACCOUNTABILITIES:
Research all customer disputes for validity by working with appropriate departments to resolve issues and prevent future non-payment deliveries
Action against Accounts Receivable department inquiries in a timely and compliant manner, utilizing location Cashiers for backup documentation needs
Proactively identify and rebill customers for owed payments
Oversee nonsufficient funds collection, processing, and submission to legal department and/or collections agency
Review daily document audits for problematic trends, communicating pricing discrepancies and other issues to account owners and management for resolution
Monitor email and phone calls; proactively respond to and/or coordinate issues when possible
Ability to work proficiently and calmly under high pressure situations
Ability to occasionally lift up to 20lbs, climb stairs, and operate in a normal office environment
Work on special projects, other miscellaneous duties, and provide backup support to other Admin as needed
LEADERSHIP BEHAVIORS:
Ownership and initiative of all AR responsibilities and processes
Demonstrate tact, highest integrity, maturity, professionalism, and respect for others
Must be a strong team player and assist other department staff when necessary
Willingness to learn and take on projects
Ability to handle multiple changing priorities simultaneously in sometimes challenging situations and keep management involved as needed
Outstanding organization and follow-up skills
