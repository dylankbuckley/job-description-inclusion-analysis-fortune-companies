COMPANY OVERVIEW:

As a part of TIAA’s Retail & Institutional Financial Services business, TIAA Bank, a division of TIAA, FSB, provides full-service, nationwide banking and lending services to consumer, commercial and institutional clients through a variety of channels, including online, its Florida-based financial centers and at other business offices throughout the country.

For more information about TIAA, visit our website.


POSITION SUMMARY:

Provide support to bank operations by opening and processing individual retirement accounts along with the disposition of decedent accounts.



KEY RESPONSIBILITIES:

Process New Account Openings

•        Prepare and analyze necessary documentation for account opening, ensure documents and forms are properly completed

•        Process account opening for individual retirement accounts and deceased disposition upon approval

•        Complete data entry of account applications through various systems

•        Verify client’s signature, identifying any discrepancy on forms using various systems

•        Participate in user acceptance testing and system deploys

Review and Process Individual Retirement Transactions

•        Decision questionable client signatures and/or discrepancy on forms

•        Follow up with applicants and/or external clients regarding missing information for clarification of instructions

•        Evaluate, analyze, and process IRA account transactions according to government regulations and company policies, keeping abreast of all regulatory changes to Individual Retirement Accounts




QUALIFICATIONS:



Required Qualifications:



•             High school diploma or equivalent required



•             1+ year of experience in banking as a teller or branch manager



•             6 months+ IRA working knowledge or experience







Preferred Qualifications:



•             Individual Retirement Account and/or deceased processing experience preferred



•             Certified IRA Service Professional preferred
