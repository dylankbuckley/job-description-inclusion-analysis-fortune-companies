Overview
The Senior Manager, Operational Communications is responsible for leading a team that creates thoughtful, engaging, clear communications for our frontline employees. They will partner with senior leaders to build and drive communications strategies that support employees to do their jobs efficiently and deliver great service

Responsibilities

Lead and manage a team responsible for the creation and implementation of operational communications
Provide communications counsel/support for top leaders and key subject matter experts
Plan and execute end-to-end campaigns that support business priorities and drive behavior change
Create, edit, manage and publish relevant content
Design a comprehensive channel strategy that enables content to reach the frontline in ways that work best for their environment
Monitor effectiveness of operational communications materials and channels; use data to make planful decisions about communications
Develop deep knowledge of frontline audiences and their communications preferences to represent their needs at the CSC (Willis Tower Corporate Support Center)
