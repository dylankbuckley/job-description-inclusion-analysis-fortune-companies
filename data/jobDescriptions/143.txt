SHIFT: Day Job

SCHEDULE: Full-time

Regulatory Affairs Analyst

This position is located in Virginia Beach, VA and is an office based position (once offices are back open due to COVID).



Responsible for ensuring and maintaining compliance with specific applicable laws and regulations impacting the business.



Primary duties may include, but are not limited to:

Keeps up-to-date on laws/regulations that impact the business, including IngenioRx (PBM) licensure requirements.
Responsible for obtaining licensure, maintaining annual license renewals and reviewing any new licensure requirements.
Responsible for applicable reporting tied to licensure requirements.
Develops and maintains working knowledge of those state and federal laws/regulations that govern the business related to licensure.
Constantly reviews information about new laws/regulations and changes in interpretation on laws/ regulations obtained from various sources related to licensure.
Ensures that all affected organizational units are informed of new laws/regulations and how they impact the business.
Produces concise and accurate written summaries of any new laws or regulations related to licensure.
Discusses new laws/regulations in a thorough, yet understandable manner.
Performs original research on points of laws that may arise as issues of concern.
Responsible for tracking and maintaining comprehensive records of all licensure filings.

Qualifications:

Requires a BA/BS; 3 years of related experience; or any combination of education and experience, which would provide an equivalent background.
Training in the legal field preferred.
Excellent verbal and written communication required.
Ability to research and summarize information
