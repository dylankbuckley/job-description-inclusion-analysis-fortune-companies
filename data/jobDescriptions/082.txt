Job Description
The CCB Dashboard Team partners closely with finance leadership, senior management, and subject matter experts across CCB to ensure successful implementation of tactical and strategic change efforts. This associate position will report to a product owner within the team responsible for supporting dashboard development across a line of business or functional area. The remit will encompass all end to end project management, prioritization, planning and execution of work inclusive of infrastructure design, test execution, and implementation. The successful candidate must possess strong analytical& problem-solving skills, have the ability to prioritize and manage time, and be able to build relationships with key stakeholders.



In this role you will be required to:

Work with key stakeholders (including business leaders, CFOs, and F&BM leadership) to determine requirements for new dashboards
Have and leverage a strong foundational knowledge of financials and financial metrics to build effective solutions that simplify, automate, and add value
Express creativity and ingenuity to transform how we see and understand data
Develop dashboards in Tableau to summarize and analyze key business drivers
Work with Technology, MIS, Analytics Teams, and Finance Teams to develop an infrastructure to support dashboards that works efficiently and has strong controls
Understand the variety of sources for information and how to strategically source data
Tracking development and deployment timelines
User Acceptance Testing and Validation of new dashboards
Support the production of newly developed dashboards and transition of production activities to Technology
Partner effectively across CCB finance, risk, marketing, and technology
Analyze customer behaviors and business results to develop insights for the CCB Leadership Team
Develop and utilize technical expertise in Alteryx and Tableau to obtain and analyze information effectively and efficiently
Qualifications

Bachelor’s degree required
2+ years of experience in finance, data & analytics, and/or project management within the Financial Services industry
Tableau and data wrangling (e.g. Alteryx) experience, is a plus
Experience with data warehouse systems, and user access, mining, and reporting applications/tools, is a plus
Flexible individual who can easily and efficiently transition across topics and disciplines
Adept at multi-tasking and meeting deadlines in high-pressure environment
Strong communication skills and the ability to synthesize work products into executive summaries and/or briefs and reports
A proven ability to take the initiative while building partnerships
Ability to define and track project deliveries through to implementation
Proactive/self-starter with the ability to deliver value-added support to business partners in a dependable, timely and accurate manner
Controls/Risk management focus - Ensure we remain adaptable and responsive to the changing regulatory landscape
Adept at data organization and maintenance – must be detail oriented
Able to work both independently and as part of a team
Excellent time management skills and highly results oriented
