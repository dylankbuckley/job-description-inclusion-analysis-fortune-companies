Job Description

In this role, specialist will be responsible for performing data attribute reviews to identify and confirm similarities and variations in a computer based system.



This is an on-site office role, however, at this time the position will start as a remote – work from home position until further notice.  Candidates will need to be prepared to work either on-site at a JPMorgan Chase facility or remote as required.



Core Responsibilities:

·         Provide critical support to several lines of business through accurate quality control, record keeping, and office support functions

·         Protect the organization by providing quality control review of data entry performed by other colleagues

·         Light material handling to include sorting and organizing color coded paper, stocking office supplies, and folding boxes

Qualifications



·         Ability to read, write, and match values.

·         Ability to perform light clerical/ data entry functions on a computer.

·         Ability to perform clerical and communication functions via virtual platforms, computer, phone etc.

·         With provided support the ability to follow instructions and procedures.
