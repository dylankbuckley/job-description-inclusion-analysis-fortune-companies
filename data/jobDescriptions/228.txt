Facebook is seeking a talented Associate General Counsel Privacy Compliance attorney to advise on the  designing and operating of a  comprehensive reporting process to enable corporate governance functions, including for the Chief Privacy Officer, Independent Privacy Assessor, and Board of Directors. Advise on reporting process, including assessment of sufficiency of content under relevant legal obligations. Provide legal guidance related to privacy compliance enforcement by supporting:  governance bodies, privacy policy setting, monitoring and reporting on privacy controls, escalated decisions on privacy issues/risks, and escalated enforcement of privacy requirements.  Handle escalated reporting issues related to assessment, certification, regulatory, or similar reporting
Associate General Counsel Privacy Compliance, Governance Responsibilities
Manage Legal’s support of key aspects of our privacy compliance programs and processes to ensure we meet our obligations under those programs
Develop and maintain subject matter expertise in areas of data use and protection laws and advising about the impact of the same on Facebook’s business
Provide Legal advice and day-to-day support to the Privacy Program’s Assessor Support Office
Investigate and advise on potential privacy issues related to corporate governance and external reporting
Provide legal advice on privacy program controls testing and management of privacy compliance
Support members of the legal, business, and policy teams on privacy related legal matters, including in areas related to partnerships, acquisitions, integrations, and other business development opportunities
Drive high-profile and high-impact projects involving privacy analysis and compliance with stakeholders across Facebook's business including Policy, Legal, Communications, Security, and other departments
Minimum Qualifications
8+ years of legal experience, including at a law firm and/or in-house experience with internet or technology companies
J.D. degree (or foreign equivalent) and membership in at least one US state bar
Experience with compliance, regulatory, or product counseling
Experience prioritizing work and providing advice under deadlines
Experience using business judgment and with client counseling
Experience advancing projects in a multi-stakeholder environment
Preferred Qualifications
Experience with global privacy laws, compliance, regulations, and industry guidelines, especially including those relevant for social media and Internet companies
Demonstrated expertise in privacy and data issues arising in one or more privacy areas such as HR, security, advertising, B2B products, Internet of Things, or privacy program implementation/management
