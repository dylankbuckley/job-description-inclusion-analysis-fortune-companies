Full Time Opportunities for Undergrad Students or Recent Graduates: Sales





We live in a mobile-first, cloud-first world, and the transformation we are driving across our businesses enables our customers to thrive in the digital world. Our sales professionals are the essential link between the potential of what's possible and build trusted advisor relationships with customers and technology vendors.



Do you want to work on a meaningful and impactful project and make a difference? Are you willing to learn from others and open to new ideas? Do you want to support others to succeed and operate in a highly collaborative and global environment? If this sounds like you, Microsoft would like to invite you to come join us as you are, where you can find more than just a job.   Read on to learn more about opportunities and apply online!  



Applications to these opportunities are considered for all available sales roles including the ones described below, depending on education level and/or location. To be considered as a student or recent graduate, you should be within 12 months of graduation or graduated within the last 12 months.



Specialist



As a Sales Specialist you identify our customers’ pain points [LM1] and solve them through the latest software and services solutions. You will be our customers’ trusted consultant, thoroughly evaluating the health of their IT platforms and development environments and diagnosing areas for improvement. Your solutions drive measurable value, helping customers run their businesses more cost-effectively and efficiently and giving them a competitive edge in their marketplace.



Responsibilities
·         You will build and maintain relationships with executive business and technical decision-makers.

·         Articulate and present the business value of Microsoft’s solutions.

·         Develop a firm understanding of Microsoft’s strategies and product offerings relative to our major competitors.


Qualifications


·         Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, Engineering, or related field

·         Less than 12 months since graduation of your bachelor’s or master’s degree.

·         Less than 5 years of professional experience

·         English – Advanced

·         Excellent verbal and written communication, analytical and presentation skills.

·         Strong desire and business acumen for consultative solution selling.

·         Exceptional negotiation, customer service, and interpersonal skills.
