Primary Responsibilities

Lead a team within Cigna Pharmacy Economics responsible for managing commercial client pricing details
Provide oversight, direction, and approval on client MAC pricing updates
Provide oversight, direction, and approval of pricing actions supporting client performance guarantees
Provide oversight, direction, and approval of Rebate POS rate updates
Provide input, direction, and support for ESI claim adjudication migration efforts

Collaborate and provide consultation to matrix areas such as Finance, Underwriting, Product, Networking, etc.
Business Partnerships

Present work to various stakeholders such as Pharmacy Finance, Performance Guarantee Unit, Pharmacy Underwriting, Go To Market/Sales
Be an advocate for the individual customer
Be a trusted expert on all things client pricing related
Staff Leadership & Development

Lead a team of actuarial students and finance personnel in achieving goals outlined above and ensure objectives are achieved in a compliant and ethical manner
Create a supportive team environment that includes training and development, regular feedback, support for exam process for AEDP students, and ongoing career mentorship
Qualifications

Six plus years of financial analysis and/or actuarial experience
Strong analytical, problem solving, and critical thinking skills
Excellent oral, written presentation, and communication skills
Ability to lead and influence in a matrix environment strongly preferred
