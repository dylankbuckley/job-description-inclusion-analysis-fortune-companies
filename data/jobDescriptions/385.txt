The details

What you’ve got




You hold a bachelor’s degree or equivalent.

You have prior experience in sales or a client service environment (preferred).

You possess solid communication skills—oral and written.

You’re a born negotiator.

You have strong interpersonal skills to build rapport with prospective and existing clients

You’re able to succeed in a semi-autonomous, fast-paced environment.

You understand and review complex coverage details and underwriting guidelines.
