As a Software Developer Lead (Technical Application Owner) in our Product Definition Tools (PDT) service management team you’ll lead the technical vision, strategy, design, transition, and operation of applications assigned to you. You’ll collaborate with the Domain Architect to create the technical vision, and strategize the desired future state; monitor application changes to ensure architecture guidelines and standards are met; oversee incident and problem management, monitor trends, and identify, propose, and execute on actions to reduce incidents and prevent problems; analyze and estimate impact of projects on application and team.

Location: Mayfield Village, OH or Colorado Springs, CO

• Ability to establish and maintain strong working relationships with Domain Architect, Service Managers, other Technical Application Owners, business stakeholders, and other IT platforms
