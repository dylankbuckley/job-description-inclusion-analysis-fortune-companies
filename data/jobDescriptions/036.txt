Position Description

The role will be responsible for leading the IT Project Management team and accountable for all aspects of the development/implementation of projects and programs involving cross-functional teams focused on the delivery of a product/program from the design process through a finished state for internal/external customers. It will be able to work with minimal direction in a fast-paced, continuously evolving environment and assist the Project Managers to implement optimal planning, decision-making, and project execution processes across the key initiatives and cross-functional teams.

Key Responsibilities:

Day to day, this role oversees and directs the activities of the Project Management team to ensure the successful completion of multiple projects and adherence to quality standards, budgetary guidelines, and timelines. Duties may include, but are not limited to:
Lead and coach the Project Management team to meet all objectives, milestones, KPIs, and OKRs
Monitor and bring visibility of the work and status of projects across the Project Management team to leadership and stakeholders
Hire, train, and manage the performance management of the Project Management team
Ensure quality standards are maintained within each project and program
Helps define and initiate policies and processes and oversee ongoing improvement
Monitor department and project level budgets and financial reporting
Coordinate the team’s efforts and plans with those of other products across the IT organization and other business groups
Collaborate with Portfolio Strategy and Product Owners/Managers to define, prioritize, track, and drive the progress of key initiatives involving the projects and programs
Work with technical teams and leaders to continually clarify and refine project objectives, acceptance criteria, budget, and workplan and procure appropriate resources (e.g., licenses, contractors, etc.)
Maintain program level project plans and tracking logs as appropriate, communicates blockers/risks, and crafts recommendations for resolutions or escalations as needed
Curate/document critical information regarding project deliverables and progress and proactively disseminates updates to leadership and stakeholders within and outside of those initiatives
Minimum Requirements:

12+ years project management experience and 4+ years of supervisory experience
Critical Skills:

6+ years’ experience managing cross-functional programs/projects
2 years’ leading people, setting direction and deploying resources
Detailed understanding of and experience with Agile project planning and management methodologies (e.g., Scrum, Scaled Agile, Kanban) and workflow process documentation and operational improvement.
Intermediate financial and statistical analysis skills
Advanced skills with technology focused project management tools—e.g., Atlassian, etc.—and Microsoft Office Suite including MS PowerPoint, MS Excel, MS Project and MS Visio
Additional Requirements:

Experience within the healthcare IT and/or healthcare field with knowledge of medical business process, operations and tools
Certification and practical experience in Agile, Lean/Six Sigma, and/or PMP
Budgetary experience
Strong written and verbal communication and customer/stakeholder management skills
Basic knowledge of EHR technology, data & analytics and architecture
