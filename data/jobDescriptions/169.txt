We are seeking somebody with passion, high integrity, great communication skills and fun-loving personality experienced as an Executive Assistant with the ability to support and coordinate projects for the SVP of a growing and critical organization within VMware. In this role you will be a key and trusted member of the staff, and instrumental in the efficient and organized management of VMware’s Customer Success business.

Responsibilities of the Executive Assistant include, but are not limited to, the following:

• Field all incoming calls

• Heavy calendaring and email management

• Coordination and planning for meetings, conference calls and special events, including arrangement of logistics, meeting space, communications and catering

• Attend and take notes during weekly meetings and follow-up on action items

• Arrange and confirm local, domestic and international travel for the Executive and other members of the organization

• Facilitate communication with all levels of management, both internal and external

• Create, edit and proofread business communications and documents utilizing MS Word, Excel and PowerPoint

• Prepare/track expense reports

• Receive visitors

• Create & track PRs & POs as required

• Special projects on an as-needed basis

• E-mail, fax, scan and copy documents

• Lead and organize planning for company-wide events

Requirements:

• Associate/Bachelor's degree preferred and at least three years of experience at a technology company

• 8 + years executive level support required, VP-level preferred for a public, Billion $+company, communication roles and project management experience are highly desirable.

• Extremely strong organizational, project and time management skills; detail-oriented

• Advanced level skills with PowerPoint, Excel, MS Word, Outlook

• Demonstrated leadership and mentoring skills

• Strong sense of urgency and efficiency in completing work

• Ability to take self-initiative and be proactive

• Ability to multi-task and prioritize workload

• Ability to recognize and appropriately handle highly sensitive and confidential material and information

• Excellent verbal and written communication skills and able to act diplomatically across teams to help the SVP drive results

• Professional demeanor and presentation
